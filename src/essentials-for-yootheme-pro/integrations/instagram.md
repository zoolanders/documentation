# Instagram Integration

<div class="tm-resource-icon">
    <!--@include: ./assets/brands/instagram.svg-->
</div>

The Instagram integration is available through [Instagram Source](../addons/dynamic/sources/instagram). Use it for personal and business accounts, with personal accounts authenticating through the [Instagram Auth Driver](../auths/drivers/instagram) and business accounts through the [Facebook Auth Driver](../auths/drivers/facebook) when the Instagram account is connected to a Facebook Page.

Instagram Business accounts must be connected to a Facebook Page, and the Facebook user must have access to that Page in Meta Business Manager.

For queries, account-specific setup, and the available media fields, see [Instagram Source](../addons/dynamic/sources/instagram).

The Vimeo integration is available through [Vimeo Source](../addons/dynamic/sources/vimeo). Use it to read videos from a Vimeo account and query videos, folders, and showcases.

For setup details, query options, and the available video fields, see the [Vimeo Source](../addons/dynamic/sources/vimeo) docs.
